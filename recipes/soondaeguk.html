Soondae Guk

Description

The star ingredient in this hearty Korean soup is sundae, or Korean blood sausage. The soup is prepared with a flavorful broth that includes sliced sundae, other types of offal, vegetables, and noodles, while rice can be added to the soup or served on the side.

Sundaeguk is regarded as an excellent hangover remedy and it is usually enjoyed with kimchi, kkakdugi, or other Korean side dishes (banchan).

    Ingredients
  • 1/2lb Soondae
  • 2 Cups Ox Bone broth
  • 1/4 Cup Garlic Chives
  • 1/4 Cup Green Onions
  • 1/4 Cup Perilla Leaves
  • 2 Tbsp Green Hot Pepper
  • 1 and 1/2 Tbsp Perilla Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Salted Shrimp
  • Optional: Spicy Seasoning Paste
    Steps
  1. Make or purchase Ox Bone Broth ahead of time.
  2. Prepare soondae according to package, then slice.
  3. Slice vegetables.
  4. Make seasoning paste, if desired.
  5. Pour bone broth into pot, and heat on high.
  6. Once boiling, add soondae into the broth. Make sure not to cook the soondae for too long or it may fall apart.
  7. Once the water is boiling again, quickly turn the heat off and toss in veggies.
  8. Put the seasoning paste on top of the veggies
